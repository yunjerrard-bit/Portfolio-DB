<title>writer.py — 6개 colored Format에 bold 추가</title>

<behavior>

- SigmaBucket.SOFT_GREEN, SOFT_RED → font_color 옆에 `"bold": True`
- SigmaBucket.HARD_GREEN, HARD_RED → font_color + bg_color 옆에 `"bold": True`
- TechBucket.SOFT_GREEN, SOFT_RED → font_color 옆에 `"bold": True`
- DEFAULT 두 종은 변경 없음
- header Format은 이미 bold (변경 없음)

</behavior>
