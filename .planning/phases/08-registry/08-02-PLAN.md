  OPM    FLOW_TTM   op_income     revenue              is_ratio_0_1=True
  ROE    HYBRID     net_income    total_equity         (D-03 분모=최근값)

  BPS    PER_SHARE  total_equity  shares_outstanding   (PBR 분모; 저량)
  SPS    PER_SHARE  revenue       shares_outstanding   (PSR 분모; 유량 분자 TTM)
  OCF_ps PER_SHARE  operating_cash_flow shares_outstanding (PCR 분모; 유량 분자 TTM)

  PBR    PER_SHARE/가격÷BPS

  PSR    PER_SHARE/가격÷SPS
